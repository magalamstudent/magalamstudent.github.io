About

Pura Desa is a type of Hindu temple found in Bali, Indonesia, a magical place nicknamed “the island of the gods.” This is my inspiration for a healthy food concept focusing on smoothie bowls and juices. The idea stems from the unique flavors of exotic tropical fruits found on the island.

Born out of a 1 month trip that ended up turning into half a year, the beauty of Bali enchants all of its visitors. The traditions of their culture are kept alive with the Balinese faithfulness to their religion. At every temple I visited, beautiful offerings would be presented by women in colorful sarongs and kebayas.

These memories I will try to keep alive with Pura Desa. Every bowl I eat or juice I drink transports me back to a time when I was younger and carefree and living in paradise. I hope to bring these wonderful flavors to you also……and perhaps inspire a future trip.
