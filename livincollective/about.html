About Us

Our humble story began in 2015, when we decided to move to the laid-back beach city of Nha Trang, Vietnam. It started as an experiment, an escape from our normal 9-5 jobs, with a dash of adventure and wanderlust. Since then, we have evolved into one of the top restaurant and shopping destinations in this beautiful paradise we call home.
